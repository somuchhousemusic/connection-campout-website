What Awaits

Three Days in the Desert

Tucked into the vast silence of Eastern Oregon's high desert, Connection Campout brings together the underground — music that moves you, art that opens you, and the healing power of ancient waters. Soak in geothermal springs under the Milky Way. Come connect.

The Place

Summer Lake Hot Springs

One of Oregon's most remote and magical landscapes. Ancient geothermal pools, volcanic ridgelines, and skies that go on forever. No light pollution. Pure high desert.

Wellness

Soak, Breathe, Restore

Natural geothermal pools, morning yoga, breathwork, sound healing, and workshops for the body and mind.

Art

Land & Living Art

Monumental installations, interactive light works, and immersive environments woven into the landscape. The desert is the canvas.

Music

The Underground, Outdoors

Deep house, bass, and everything in between — curated for the open sky and the sagebrush plains. Multiple stages from sunset to sunrise.

The Place

Summer Lake Hot Springs

A working geothermal resort in the high desert of Lake County, Oregon. Historic bathhouse, outdoor pools, stone soaking tubs, a stone labyrinth — and nothing but open sky and silence in every direction.

Every Cabin Includes

  • Geothermally-heated floors
  • Full kitchen
  • Outdoor fire pit
  • French press, electric kettle & coffee grinder
  • Electric fans (summer) / portable heaters (winter)
  • Hand towels & kitchen cloths provided
  • Fitted sheet per bed provided

Please Bring Your Own Bedding

To support our eco-conscious approach, cabin guests must bring sheets, pillows, blankets, and bath towels. We provide a fitted sheet per bed, hand towels, and kitchen cloths. Thank you for helping us minimize water and energy use in the Oregon Outback.

☕ Coffee, the Outback Way — Each cabin has a French press, electric kettle, and coffee grinder. Grind your beans, brew slow, and embrace the pace of the high desert.
